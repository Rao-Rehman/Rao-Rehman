ROLLS-ROYCE

Rolls-Royce Phantom

The Phantom is the epitome of automotive luxury, where every detail is handcrafted to perfection. Its presence commands attention, not with noise, but with grace. Riding in one feels less like travel and more like a ceremony. The 2024 model features enhanced sound insulation, a redesigned "Gallery" dashboard for custom artwork, and the latest iteration of Rolls-Royce's 6.75-liter V12 engine producing 563 horsepower.

Top Speed: 155 mph

0-60 mph: 5.1s

Starting at: $460,000

LAMBORGHINI

Lamborghini Revuelto

The Revuelto is Lamborghini's first V12 hybrid supercar, combining raw power with cutting-edge technology. Its 6.5-liter V12 engine works in harmony with three electric motors to produce a combined 1,001 horsepower. The striking design features Lamborghini's signature scissor doors and an aerodynamically optimized body that generates significant downforce. The 2024 model introduces a new infotainment system with 3D graphics and enhanced driver assistance features.

Top Speed: 217 mph

0-60 mph: 2.5s

Starting at: $608,358

FERRARI

Ferrari Purosangue

Ferrari's first four-door, four-seater model combines supercar performance with SUV practicality. Powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine producing 715 horsepower, the Purosangue features rear-hinged back doors and an innovative active suspension system. The 2024 model offers enhanced all-wheel-drive capabilities while maintaining Ferrari's signature driving dynamics. The interior features premium materials with extensive customization options.

Top Speed: 193 mph

0-60 mph: 3.2s

Starting at: $398,350

BENTLEY

Bentley Continental GT Speed

The Continental GT Speed represents the pinnacle of Bentley's grand touring capabilities. Its 6.0-liter W12 twin-turbocharged engine delivers 650 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, propelling the car from 0-60 mph in just 3.5 seconds. The 2024 model features enhanced aerodynamics, a redesigned grille, and the latest iteration of Bentley's rotating display that can switch between touchscreen, analog dials, or a clean veneer panel.

Top Speed: 208 mph

0-60 mph: 3.5s

Starting at: $245,000

ASTON MARTIN

Aston Martin DB12

The DB12 is Aston Martin's latest grand tourer, combining breathtaking design with formidable performance. Its 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 produces 671 horsepower, making it the most powerful DB model yet. The 2024 version features a completely redesigned interior with a new infotainment system developed in-house, along with enhanced aerodynamics and a retuned suspension for improved handling without sacrificing comfort.

Top Speed: 202 mph

0-60 mph: 3.5s

Starting at: $248,086
